Welcome to the EL CAMINO Fan Page!

El Camino: A Breaking Bad Movie is a 2019 American neo-Western crime thriller film. It's about Jesse Pinkman (Aaron Paul) who must come to terms with his past after escaping captivity. The movie was written and directed by Vince Gilligan, the creator of Breaking Bad.

Discover exclusive posters, in-depth character bios, and test your knowledge with our trivia quiz. Join us in celebrating this cinematic masterpiece!

Character Bios

Aaron Paul as Jesse Pinkman

Jesse is a crystal meth cook and dealer who works with his former high school chemistry teacher, Walter White. He is introduced as a small-time drug dealer and crystal meth junkie, but as the series progresses, more of his personality emerges, and it's revealed that he's not as bad as other characters in the show.

Krysten Ritter as Jane Margoliss

Jane is shown to be a highly intelligent, caring and loyal young woman who is a skilled, creative artist and is overall a good person at heart. Jane, like her eventual boyfriend Jesse Pinkman, has not had an easy life due to strong parental interference which was a result of drug use.

Jonathan Banks as Mike Ehrmantraut

Mike is a former police officer and Marine who works as a private investigator, hitman, and more for Gus Fring and Saul Goodman. Mike's past corrupt activities led to the death of his son, Matt, and he moves to Albuquerque to support his daughter-in-law and granddaughter.

Jesse Plemons as Todd Alquist

Todd Alquist is a methamphetamine cook and former exterminator for Vamonos Pest, and the nephew of Jack Welker. He worked for Walter White, Mike Ehrmantraut, and Jesse Pinkman in their meth manufacturing business.

Charles Baker as Skinny pete

Skinny Pete is a friend of Jesse Pinkman who is recruited to sell crystal meth on behalf of Walter White.

Bryan Cranston as Walter White

Walter White is a high school chemistry teacher in Albuquerque, New Mexico, who turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamines after being diagnosed with terminal lung cancer.

Henry R. as Hank Schrader

Hank is a DEA agent in Albuquerque, New Mexico who leads the investigation into the methamphetamine cook "Heisenberg". Hank is unaware that Heisenberg is actually his brother-in-law, Walter White.

Giancarlo Esposito as Gustavo Fring

Gus Fring is a drug kingpin and fast-food restaurateur who is portrayed by Giancarlo Esposito. He is known for his ruthless nature and his desire to create a new "family" through his meth empire and chicken restaurants.

Bob Odenkirk as Saul Goodman

Saul is a self-centered and unscrupulous Albuquerque-based lawyer who embraces his tactics as a former con artist and becomes involved in the city's criminal underworld.
